## Analysis

- Lowering matrix LR from 0.08 to 0.07 hurt performance (1.0387 → 1.0473, +0.0086)
- The 12.5% LR reduction slowed weight updates for the Muon-optimized matrix parameters, preventing them from reaching good solutions in the 5-minute budget
- This regression magnitude (+0.0086) is comparable to Session 13's increase to 0.1 (+0.0026), confirming 0.08 is a local optimum — though going lower hurts more than going higher
- Throughput was similar (1361 steps, 14.26% MFU), so the regression is purely from suboptimal learning dynamics
- **Conclusion: MATRIX_LR=0.08 is confirmed optimal. Both 0.07 and 0.1 are worse. The Muon LR parameter space is fully explored (0.07 < 0.08 > 0.1).**
