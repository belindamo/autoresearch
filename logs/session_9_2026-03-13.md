**Reduce weight decay from 0.05 to 0.025**

Rationale: Sessions 7 and 8 showed successive improvements from reducing weight decay (0.2→0.1→0.05), suggesting the model benefits from less regularization in short 5-minute runs. This experiment pushes further to see if 0.025 continues the trend.

## Analysis

- Reducing weight decay from 0.05 to 0.025 hurt performance (1.0395 → 1.0437)
- Steps decreased (1517→1418) and MFU dropped (15.91%→14.85%), tokens processed also fell (198.8M→185.9M)
- The diminishing returns from weight decay reduction have turned negative — 0.05 appears to be the sweet spot
- Too little regularization likely allows the model to overfit to recent batches, hurting generalization
- The cautious WD schedule (`WD * (1 - progress)`) means even at 0.05 the WD decays to zero by end of training — going lower primarily starves the early training phases of useful regularization
- **Conclusion: 0.05 is the optimal weight decay for 5-minute runs; further reduction is counterproductive**
