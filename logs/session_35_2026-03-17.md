## Analysis

- All six experiments regressed, continuing the pattern from sessions 31-34
- The model remains deeply optimized at **1.0387** for this GPU/time budget
- **EMA decay is a critical hyperparameter**: 0.995 caused the largest regression (+0.025) because the averaging window becomes too large relative to total training steps
- **Muon optimizer is well-tuned**: momentum target (0.95), beta2 (0.95), and existing schedules are all near-optimal
- **Architecture is stable**: MLP expansion (4x) and window pattern (SSSL) are confirmed optimal; changes in either direction degrade performance
- **GPU variance dominates**: seed 137 at ~14% MFU gives 1.054, while all-L at ~17% MFU gives 1.049 ‚ÄĒ a 0.005 gap from GPU speed alone
- The best result (1.0387) remains from session 15 at 16.35% MFU; beating it likely requires either a genuinely novel approach or a similarly fast GPU allocation

## Key confirmed values this session

- EMA decay: **0.99** >> 0.995 (effective window too large for short training)
- Muon momentum target: 0.90 < **0.95** > 0.975 (fully mapped)
