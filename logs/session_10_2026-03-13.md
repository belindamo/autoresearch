# Session 10 — 2026-03-13

## Experiment

**Increase logit softcap from 15 to 30**

Rationale: The logit softcap constrains the output logit range via `softcap * tanh(logits / softcap)`. At softcap=15, logits are clamped to [-15, 15]. Doubling to 30 allows more dynamic range, potentially helping the model express sharper probability distributions when confident, reducing cross-entropy loss.

## Changes

- `softcap`: 15 → 30 (in `GPT.forward()`)

## Analysis

- Increasing softcap from 15 to 30 hurt performance (1.0395 → 1.0440)
- Despite more steps (1486 vs 1517) and higher MFU (15.58% vs 15.91%), the wider logit range did not help
- The tanh softcap at 15 provides beneficial implicit regularization of logits — loosening it allows logits to grow larger, which may cause:
    - Gradient instability from sharper softmax distributions
  - The model spending capacity learning to produce extreme logits rather than better representations
- The original softcap=15 appears well-tuned for this model size and training duration
- **Conclusion: softcap=15 is the right setting; widening the logit range is counterproductive**
