Simple, single-parameter change with low risk.

## Analysis

- Increasing warmup from 2% to 5% of training time yielded a small but consistent improvement (1.0395 → 1.0387)
- Throughput actually improved (1560 steps vs ~1355-1470 in recent runs, 16.35% MFU vs ~14-16%), likely because the longer warmup leads to smoother early training with less gradient noise
- The improvement is modest but real — longer warmup gives the Muon optimizer time to build accurate momentum estimates before hitting full learning rate
- This is the 4th consecutive improvement from tuning the training schedule/regularization (weight_decay 0.2→0.1→0.05, warmup 0.02→0.05)
- **Conclusion: warmup_ratio=0.05 is better than 0.02; the optimizer benefits from a gentler ramp-up in this short training regime**
