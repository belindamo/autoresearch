## Experiment

**Increase matrix LR from 0.08 to 0.1**

Rationale: The Muon optimizer's matrix LR was successfully increased from 0.06 to 0.08 in Session 5. Pushing it further to 0.1 could allow the bulk of the model's parameters (attention and MLP matrices) to learn faster within the 5-minute training budget.

## Analysis

- Increasing matrix LR from 0.08 to 0.1 hurt performance (1.0395 → 1.0413)
- The regression is modest (0.0018) but clear — the Muon optimizer is already near its optimal LR at 0.08
- Higher matrix LR likely causes overshooting during the warmdown phase, where the cosine schedule brings the LR down — starting from a higher peak means the effective LR stays too high for longer
- Throughput dropped slightly (1504 → 1473 steps, 197.1M → 193.1M tokens) which is within noise but may indicate slightly less stable training
- **Conclusion: matrix_lr=0.08 is optimal; do not increase further. The 0.06→0.08 improvement does not extrapolate to 0.1**
