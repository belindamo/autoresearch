**Increase unembedding LR from 0.004 to 0.008**

Rationale: The unembedding (lm_head) learning rate has been at 0.004 since baseline and was never tuned. It's currently the most conservative LR in the config. Doubling it could let the output projection learn faster within the 5-minute training budget.

## Analysis

- Doubling the unembedding LR from 0.004 to 0.008 significantly hurt performance (1.0395 → 1.0468)
- The regression of 0.0073 is the largest among recent single-parameter changes, suggesting the lm_head is quite sensitive to LR
- The lm_head is initialized with very small weights (std=0.001) and uses AdamW — a higher LR likely causes the output logits to overshoot early in training, destabilizing the softcap dynamics
- Throughput was also slightly lower (1355 steps vs typical ~1470+), possibly due to less stable gradients
- **Conclusion: unembedding_lr=0.004 is well-calibrated; do not increase. If anything, a *****decrease***** might be worth exploring, but the current value appears near-optimal.**
