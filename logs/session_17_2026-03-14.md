## Experiment

**Lower softcap from 15 to 10**

Rationale: Session 10 showed that increasing softcap from 15→30 hurt performance (+0.0053). The hypothesis here is that tighter logit clamping (softcap=10) could provide stronger regularization that benefits short training runs. Simple, single-parameter change.

## Analysis

- Lowering softcap from 15 to 10 slightly hurt performance (1.0387 → 1.0408, +0.0021)
- The tighter logit clamp constrains the model's output distribution too aggressively, preventing it from expressing confident predictions
- With softcap=10, logits are clamped to [-10, 10] which limits the dynamic range for a 32K vocabulary — the model needs more expressiveness to discriminate between tokens
- Both directions have now been tested: 15→30 hurt more (+0.0053), 15→10 also hurt (+0.0021), confirming **softcap=15 is well-tuned**
- Throughput was comparable (1516 steps, 15.89% MFU), so this was purely a model quality issue
- **Conclusion: softcap=15 is optimal. Both higher (30) and lower (10) values degrade performance. The softcap parameter space is now fully explored.**
